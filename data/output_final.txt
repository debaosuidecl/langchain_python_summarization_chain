First of all, the smoker works as illustrated. Results were perfect Now for the problem I encountered. When the auger was feeding the pellets, it was making a thump sound. When it was not turning ,everything appeared fine. I called Pitboss ,and after 20 minutes, I finally could talk to a representative. I explained the problem to a lady, unfortunately, I did not ask her name. She had to discuss the issue with another, and it was decided that the auger was the problem. She sent a new one and it was received in two days. I was asked to do the repair. She sent me a video to follow . Under warranty and I have to do the labor? I am 76 years old, so I layed on the floor to remove the case hardened 4 screws on the bottom of the smoker ,As many people have noted, those screws do not remove easily. Before I stripped them, I had to resort to a screw driver and hammer to break the grip. The video then guides you along to remove the power cord grommet and then disconnect 6 connectors in the box and then 4 screws , two on each side to remove the cover. Before I started disconnecting, I went on You Tube. All I had to do was remove the 4 screws and proceed to the video Pitboss sent. All that work for no reason. I replaced the auger and guess what, same problem existed. I called again, this time , I had to wait for only 10 minutes. I spoke to John who was extremely helpful. I give him 10 stars, not 5. He discussed the issue with his team and the issue was with the pellets, not the smoker. I used pellets that contain some larger pieces. The auger was breaking these up causing the thump sound. I ordered Pitboss pellets and feel comfortable that was the issue because this morning, I vacuumed all the remaining pellets and fired up the smoker. I pressed the feeder until the auger was clear of pellets. Great news, it worked as expected. I would give the smoker a 5 rating but because of the scenario mentioned, I gave it a 4 star. Customer service was great, but the issue could have been resolved so easily. Lesson to those out there, watch what kind of pellets you are using in your Pitboss. Update---- Added the Pitboss pellets as suggested by them. The pellets did not work, and the thumping continued. So I called Pitboss again and was on hold for 35 minutes this time. I brought my phone to the smoker and had the lady listen to the thumping. She heard it three times and went to her team for direction. She asked that I take a video of it, call them again and then send the video. I questioned that my word and your word from hearing was not good enough for them? Conclusion: I am disassembling this smoker and sending it back and will purchase a Traeger.

mrussn13
Good, solid unit.
Reviewed in the United States on February 7, 2023
Style: 440D2Verified Purchase
Packaging arrived very damaged. Broken straps and gouges in the cardboard probably from someone moving it too carelessly on a forklift or with a hand truck. Got lucky and found no damaged parts, so there was no need to return it. Assembly was easy and to the point and the unit itself seems to be quality made, sturdy and very well built.

Initially had a minor temperature probe connection issue that was solved easily via advice from Pit Boss customer service. I now have 6 cooks on it ranging from burgers, to whole chicken, wild game and pizza. This grill functions as advertised and in the many youtube vids and reviews. Overall, I'm very impressed with how well built this model is. It's heavier gauge steel than the comparable Traeger models yet ranges about 25-30% less in cost.

In regards to cooking temperature- After many cooks and a few months of use, I've found that the temp shown on the digital settings panel is posting about 100-120 degrees hotter than it actually is in the chamber. Aftermarket plug in to the unit meat probes were the same. The hottest (actual) temp I can get it to is about 375 degrees. So regardless of outside temps, plan on cooler internal temps than posted and use a separate, non plug in type meat thermometer for accuracy. Easy work around for me and I simply plan for longer cook times. It's a 5 star rating unit, but leaving it at 4 due to the wide temperature differences.
